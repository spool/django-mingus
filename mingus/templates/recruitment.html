Auditions

First round acting auditions will be held over 2nd and 3rd weeks of Michaelmas Term (17 - 24 October). Please send the following information to auditions@adreamplay.com:

  • Full Name
  • University/Institution/College (if at Oxford)
  • Course/Occupation
  • If at University, what year of course
  • Mobile (in case audition slots change)
  • Acting Experience
  • Musical Instruments/Singing and Grades
  • Dance Experience
  • Preferred Audition Time

Audition times will be the afternoons of the Sunday and Saturday of 2nd week (17th and 23rd), the Sunday of 3rd week (24th), and evenings of those weeks: 18th-22nd and 25th-29th. Please indicate at least 3 preferred dates.

Please prepare and memorize a short monologue of your own choosing; something you are comfortable with and shows your strengths as an actor. It can be modern, classic, comedy or tragedy; Shakespeare is often a good choice. The director (Griffith Rees) and assistant director (Ollo Clark) will work with the audtionee to see how well they take direction.

Musicians interested in playing in the orchestra should email the director. Those auditions will be held later in term.
