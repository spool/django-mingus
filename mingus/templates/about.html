A Dream Play

A Dream Play is the story of Agnes, daughter of a god, who is fascinated by humanity from her heavenly home. Her father decides to take her to Earth in a dream, and there she meets an ensemble of characters who tell her their stories through a series of vignettes, some incorporating movement and dance. Through this dream Agnes sees what it would be like to grow up, and both how hard and joyful life can be.
